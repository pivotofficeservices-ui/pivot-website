Meet Rachel

Dallas-raised, three decades inside offices of every size — and one honest realization. That's how Pivot began.

I'm Rachel Hartman. Dallas-raised, UNT-educated, and back home in Big D after a few years in Scottsdale.

I've spent nearly three decades inside offices of every size: managing the finances and technology for a family-owned consulting firm, supporting executives from the C-suite at Omni Hotels to EVP level at Michaels, and developing private-brand products for mass-market retail.

Pivot began with an honest realization. Everywhere I looked, businesses were paying premium rates for inexperienced help, and owners were surrendering their best hours to paperwork. The winemaker stuck at a desk instead of out in the vines. The manager who just needs an extra pair of hands to carry a big project across the finish line. Time is the one thing a business can't buy back, so I built a business that gives it back: your hours return to the work you actually love. That's Pivot — the expertise without the markup, or the overhead of a full-time hire.

Beyond that? I'm the geek who genuinely enjoys getting your Wi-Fi working, showing you what your iPhone can really do, and passing along the little shortcuts that make everyday computer work easier. But underneath all of it is something simpler: I care about people and their happiness. I'm the sort who once hand-made birthday cards for every senior on my Meals on Wheels route. Helping people work better isn't just my business. It's my nature.

— Rachel Hartman, Owner
